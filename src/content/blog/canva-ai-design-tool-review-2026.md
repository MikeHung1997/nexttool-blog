## What Canva AI Actually Does

Canva is no longer just a drag-and-drop template tool. It now includes a suite of AI-powered features embedded directly into the design workflow:

- **Magic Design**: Describe what you want, upload a photo or brand assets, and Canva generates a full design layout — not just a template suggestion, but a populated, ready-to-edit design.
- **Magic Write**: An AI text assistant built into your canvas. Write captions, headlines, presentation copy, or social media text without leaving Canva.
- **Magic Edit and Magic Eraser**: Retouch photos, remove backgrounds, swap elements, or erase distractions from images using natural language or simple brush tools.
- **Text to Image**: Generate original AI images inside your design — useful when stock photos aren't quite right.
- **AI Presentation Maker**: Describe your topic, and Canva builds a full slide deck — structure, visuals, and copy — ready to refine.
- **Magic Animate and Video tools**: Apply smart animations to any design, and create short-form video content with transitions, timing, and on-brand assets.

The throughline is speed. These tools are not about replacing creative judgment — they're about eliminating the mechanical work that kills momentum.

## The Free Plan: What You Actually Get

Canva's free tier is genuinely useful — not a crippled demo. You get:

- Access to hundreds of thousands of templates

For someone just getting started — building a simple social media presence or creating occasional graphics — the free plan is enough. The AI features are limited but present.

**Best for**: Beginners, occasional users, students, or anyone who wants to test the platform before committing.

## What Canva AI Does Not Replace

Honesty matters here. Canva AI is excellent at speed and volume. It is not a replacement for:

- **Complex custom illustration** — AI-generated images inside Canva are useful, but not a substitute for a skilled illustrator on a brand identity project.
- **Advanced video editing** — For serious video production, you'll still need dedicated tools. Canva handles short-form and social video well, not feature-length or complex edits.
- **Deep brand strategy** — Canva helps you execute. It doesn't help you decide what your brand should feel like.

For the vast majority of people reading this, those limitations don't matter. The bottleneck isn't complex custom illustration — it's getting a clean, professional-looking post out the door today.
