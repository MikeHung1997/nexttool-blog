## What to Look for in AI Transcription Tools

Before we get into specific recommendations, here's what separates great transcription tools from mediocre ones:

- **Accuracy**: Top tools achieve 95%+ accuracy even with multiple speakers and accents
- **Real-time processing**: Live transcription during meetings, not just post-recording
- **Integration**: Works with your existing tools (Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, etc.)
- **Speaker identification**: Automatically labels who said what
- **Smart summaries**: Generates action items and key takeaways
- **Search functionality**: Quickly find specific moments in long recordings
